About Us

Welcome to *Smart shelf*, a platform dedicated to reducing food waste in the retail industry. We aim to revolutionize the way food retailers manage their inventories by providing tools that identify near-expiry items and allow them to take action quickly.

Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce food loss and waste across retail businesses. We believe that by harnessing technology, retailers can make more informed decisions and help minimize the environmental impact of food waste.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the leading platform for food loss management by providing innovative solutions that help businesses reduce waste, optimize inventory management, and support local communities.

Innovation

We are committed to using technology to create innovative solutions that address food loss and waste in the retail sector.

Sustainability

We prioritize sustainable practices to ensure that our platform contributes to a cleaner, greener planet.

Community

We aim to empower businesses and communities by providing the tools they need to fight food loss and waste together.
